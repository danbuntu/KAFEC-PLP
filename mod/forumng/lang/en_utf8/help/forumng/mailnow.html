Mail now

The Mail now option (which is usually not available to students) causes the system to send your post to email subscribers more quickly.

Choosing Mail now does not send your post by email to more people, or send a copy to you. It only causes existing emails, which would have been sent anyway, to be sent more quickly.

  • Ordinarily the system waits a certain time before mailing posts, in case the author decides to edit the post. The time delay is the same as the editing timeout, set in admin settings.
  • If you set Mail now then the system does not intentionally wait at all and any emails will be included in the next batch. On most systems this will still involve a short delay (usually between five minutes and half an hour).
