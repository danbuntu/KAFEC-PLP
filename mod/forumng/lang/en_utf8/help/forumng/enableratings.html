ForumNG

Enable ratings

If enabled, forum posts can be given ratings, either numeric (0-N) or using custom textual scales defined in Moodle settings. Numeric ratings are displayed as stars if the range is 0-5 or less.

Even if you enable ratings, the capabilities system controls who is allowed to rate posts and who is allowed to see the ratings. By default, students cannot rate posts, and cannot see ratings except on their own posts.

If enabled, multiple people can rate a single post. The displayed rating for a post is the average (mean) of these ratings.

There are three common use cases for the rating system:

  • Teachers rate posts for assessment purposes. The ratings might be visible to the student being assessed, or might be entirely hidden. (See Grading setting.)
  • Teachers rate posts to indicate when students make an important (or unhelpful) contribution to the discussion.
  • Students rate each others' posts to start flame wars help improve the quality of discussion.

The three capabilities involved in the rating system might be set differently for each of these cases. They are:

  • mod/forumng:rate - Able to rate posts.
  • mod/forumng:viewrating - Able to view the rating of your own posts. (By default, students have this capability.)
  • mod/forumng:viewanyrating - Able to view the rating of all posts.
